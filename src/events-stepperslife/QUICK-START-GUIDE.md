## üí≥ Payment Options (When You're Ready)

### Option 1: CASH-ONLY (No Setup Required)

**Perfect for:**
- First-time organizers
- Small local events
- Testing the platform
- Events with guaranteed attendance

**How it works:**
1. Customers reserve tickets online (free)
2. Get confirmation with QR code
3. Pay cash at the door
4. Staff scans QR code for check-in

**Requirements:**
- ‚úÖ None! Just use your 1000 free tickets

---

### Option 2: PREPAY + Online Payments

**Perfect for:**
- Events needing online ticket sales
- Professional events
- Advance ticket sales

**How it works:**
1. Use your 1000 free tickets (or buy more at $0.30/ticket)
2. Connect Stripe OR PayPal
3. Customers pay online
4. You keep 100% of ticket revenue (minus payment processing only)

**Requirements:**
- ‚úÖ Connect Stripe Connect (5 min setup)
- OR ‚úÖ Connect PayPal (when available)

**Fees:**
- Platform: $0 (already paid $0.30/ticket upfront)
- Stripe/PayPal: 2.9% + $0.30 per transaction

**Example ($25 ticket):**
```
Customer pays: $25.73
‚îú‚îÄ Ticket price: $25.00
‚îî‚îÄ Processing fee: $0.73 (2.9% + $0.30)

You paid upfront: $0.30 per ticket
You receive: $25.00 (100% of ticket price)
Your net: $24.70 per ticket
```

### Option 3: CREDIT_CARD Split Payment (No Upfront Cost)

**Perfect for:**

- Platform: 3.7% + $1.79 per ticket

## ‚ùì Common Questions

**Q: Do I need Stripe or PayPal to create an event?**
A: No! You can create events without any payment processor.

**Q: What happens if I don't set up payment?**
A: Event stays in DRAFT mode. You can use it as "Save the Date" or set up payment later.

**Q: Are the 1000 free tickets really free?**
A: Yes! Every new organizer gets 1000 tickets for their first event absolutely free.

**Q: Can I collect payments in cash?**
A: Yes! Use PREPAY model with CASH-ONLY customer payment method. No processor needed.

**Q: What if I run out of free tickets?**
A: Buy more credits at $0.30/ticket, or switch to CREDIT_CARD model (no upfront cost).

**Q: Can I change payment models later?**
A: Each event has one payment model. Create new events to try different models.

**Q: Which is better: Stripe or PayPal?**
A: Both work identically for split payments. Choose based on your preference.

**Q: Can I use both CASH and online payments?**
A: Yes! With PREPAY model, you can enable multiple customer payment methods.
